The Process

What to Expect in a Session

Each session is different — tailored entirely to you. Here's a general sense of how a typical coaching session unfolds.

Check-In

We start by reconnecting — how are you since we last spoke? What's been happening? What's on your mind?

Focus

Together we identify the most useful area to explore in this session and set a clear intention for our time.

Explore

Through conversation, reflection, and powerful questions, we dig beneath the surface to find clarity and new perspectives.

Action

We close with clear, meaningful commitments — steps you'll take before the next session to keep momentum going.
