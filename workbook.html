Who this is for

This is for you if you have finished treatment and are wondering what comes next.

You have been through something enormous. The NHS got you through it. But now the appointments are fewer, the support has stepped back, and you are left standing in a space nobody quite prepared you for.

Not ill. Not fully well. Somewhere in between.

You are allowed to want more than just survival. This workbook is the space to work out what that actually looks like for you.

What is inside

A coaching experience in a document

This is not a guide to read and put down. Every step has a personal note from Marcelo, two exercises that go straight to the heart of what you are actually going through, and a commitment section so something real changes each week.

1

Allow Yourself to Feel

Stop performing. Start telling the truth, at least to yourself.

2

Understand Your Fear

Name what is really going on underneath the scan anxiety and the "I'm fine."

3

Rebuild Your Identity

Work out who you are choosing to become, not who you were before.

4

Reconnect

With the people who matter. With your purpose. With your own body.

5

Design Your Fuller Life

Give yourself permission to want more than just survival.

About the author

Written by someone who has been through it. Twice.

My name is Marcelo Marques. I am a resilience and remission coach based in London, and I have been in remission twice.

I know what it feels like to finish treatment and not recognise the person looking back at you. I know the gap between what you say and what you actually feel. I know the fear that settles in after the scans, quieter than during treatment but just as persistent.

I wrote this workbook because nobody gave it to me when I needed it. And because I have seen, again and again, that when someone is properly supported through the remission journey, something remarkable becomes possible.
