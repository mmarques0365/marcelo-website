About Marcelo

A coach who brings both professional expertise and personal understanding to every session.

His Story

From Recovery to Purpose

After experiencing the profound impact that resilience coaching had on his own recovery journey, Marcelo Marques dedicated himself to helping others navigate life's most challenging transitions. With a background in certified life coaching and a deep specialism in resilience and remission coaching, Marcelo brings both professional expertise and personal understanding to every session.

Marcelo's approach is warm, structured, and strengths-based. He believes that every person already holds the resources they need to heal and grow — coaching simply creates the space to access them. He works with clients across the UK and internationally, primarily online, offering flexible and confidential 1:1 sessions.

His work is particularly focused on two areas: supporting professionals facing life's greatest challenges through resilience coaching, and walking alongside those in cancer remission as they rediscover their identity, purpose and confidence. Marcelo helps his clients move from fear and uncertainty towards acceptance, strength, and fulfilment.

Whether you're facing the first anxious steps of recovery, navigating a return to work, or rebuilding a life that feels meaningful again — Marcelo is here to walk that journey with you.

The Approach

Personal Philosophy

Marcelo's coaching philosophy is rooted in the belief that adversity, while painful, carries within it the seeds of profound transformation. He doesn't believe in rushing the process — real change takes time, courage, and the right support.

Drawing on positive psychology, strengths-based coaching, and mindfulness principles, Marcelo creates a safe, non-judgemental space where clients can explore their experience honestly and begin to build a new vision for their lives.

His sessions are collaborative, not prescriptive. Marcelo doesn't have all the answers — but he knows the right questions to ask. Together, coach and client discover what truly matters and chart a course towards it.

  • 💡
    Presence-Centred Full attention in every session. You are always the priority.
  • 🌱
    Growth-Oriented Focused on possibility, not limitation. What can be built from here?
  • 🔑
    Client-Led You set the agenda. Marcelo provides the structure, questions, and accountability.
  • ❤️
    Compassionate Challenge Warmth and empathy combined with honest, productive challenge.
